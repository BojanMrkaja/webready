Our Process

  • PLANNING

  • DESIGN

  • DEVELOPMENT

  • DEPLOYMENT / MAINTENACE

STEP 1:PLANNING

In the planning phase, we will gather information together, define goals and tasks. Based on the collected information, make a plan of activities in order to achieve the best possible results.

STEP 2:DESIGN

we will build a visual identity that is in line with the needs of your company and its field of activity. We will design your website from scratch including page structure, layout, navigation, colors, images and fonts. We will achieve all this on the basis of what was agreed from step 1, which is the planning phase

STEP 3:DEVELOPMENT

In the phase of development and programming of your website based on the design we have made together, we will work on creating an interactive, fast and above all functional web presentation, e-shop, blog ... all in accordance with your requirements and needs

STEP 4:DEPLOYMENT / MAINTENACE

After the completion of the development of your website and if we are sure that everything is working properly on all popular web browsers, we start uploading your website to the server. Which means that it will be available to your end users.
